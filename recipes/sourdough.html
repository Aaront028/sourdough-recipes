Sourdough Pizza Crust

Great sourdough pizza dough to top with your favorites.

Delicious sourdough pizza, cooked best in hot pizza oven or at highest temperature

Ingredients

  • 150g sourdough starter
  • 500g type 00 flour
  • 300g water
  • 15g salt
  1. Mix starter, flour, water, and salt together in a bowl. Fold every 15 minutes 3 times until dough is smooth then cover and let it sit in fridge over night.
  2. Take out the dough from the fridge and leave in room temperature for a few hours. Then cut into 4 balls.
  3. preheat oven to 260 degrees celcius
  4. Flour your surface roll out the dough or stretch to large pizza, about the size of your baking pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until pale golden, about 7 minutes.
