Sourdough Soft Pretzels

Addictive sourdough soft pretzels!

Soft, tasty hand-rolled and twisted soft pretzels made with the touch of sourdough.

Ingredients

  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 255g water
  • 40g honey
  • 10g salt
  • 500g flour

Water bath ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar

Egg wash

  • 1 large egg or olive oil (if vegetarian)
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt

Steps

  1. Mix the sourdough starter, water, honey and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Add the flour and use your hands to bring the ingredients together as much as possible.
  3. The dough should be nice and stiff, use a dough hook on the mixer and mix the dough on lowest speed for about 6 to 7 minutes or hand knead for 10 minutes.
  4. Cover the bowl and let it rest at room temperature for 8 to 12 hours
  5. Use a clean work surface and divide 8 to 16 pieces depending on the size you want.
  6. Roll out each piece to a long rope and make a "U" shape. Lift the ends of the rope and twist 2 times, fold the twist over and press the ends on the dough to create the pretzel shape. Cover the dough with a towel and let rise for 30 to 60 minutes or until puffy.
  7. Preheat oven to 218 degrees celcius. Boil the water in a large stockpot and add your baking soda and dark brown surgar.
  8. Boil the pretzels, 3 - 4 at a time, on both sides for 30 seconds each, making sure not to crowd the pot. use a mesh strainer to remove the pretzels and place back on the baking paper. Brush each pretzel with egg wash and sprinkle with salt.
  9. Bake the pretzels at 218 degrees for 12 - 15 minutes or until golden brown.
