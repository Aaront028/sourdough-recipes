Sourdough Pancakes

Crispy sourdough pancakes with blueberries

Easy sourdough pancakes you can use with your sourdough starter discard

Ingredients

  • 200g water
  • 200g flour
  • 150-200g starter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • oil or butter for panfrying

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together and leave overnight in room temperature
  2. Mix in 2 eggs to the mixture
  3. Mix in your blueberries
  4. Mix in baking soda and salt
  5. Wait for 10 minutes
  6. oil or butter and heat over medium-high heat. Working in batches, drop batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto the hot pan. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook until golden brown, another 2 to 3 minutes.
